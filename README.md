# ঋণমুক্তি — RinMukti
### Financial Awareness Platform for Bangladesh

🌐 **Live Site:** https://arafat475.github.io/-RinMukti/

---

## Why I Built This

Many families in Bangladesh go bankrupt because of loans they didn't fully understand.
They don't know the real cost, the risks, or whether they even needed the loan in the
first place. I built RinMukti to raise awareness and help people make informed financial
decisions before it's too late.

---

## What It Does

**🧮 ঋণ ক্যালকুলেটর — Loan Calculator**
Calculate the real cost of any loan — monthly EMI, total interest, total repayment,
and extra cost percentage. Includes an automatic danger popup for high interest rates
common among NGOs and MFIs in Bangladesh. Share results instantly via WhatsApp.

**📋 আমার কি ঋণ দরকার? — Do I Need This Loan?**
A 6-question quiz that helps people honestly assess whether they truly need a loan,
with a scored verdict at the end.

**⚠️ ঝুঁকি সতর্কতা — Loan Danger Guide**
5-section education page explaining how NGO loan traps work, real statistics about
debt in Bangladesh, warning signs, safer alternatives, and actionable steps for
people already in debt.

**📖 আসল গল্প — Real Stories**
Documented real cases of Bangladeshi families affected by microloan debt traps —
Shahidul Islam from Gaibandha, Joba Rani from Jamlabaj, and others.

**🇧🇩 Bangla Mode**
Full Bangla toggle for low-literacy users — hides all English text with one click.

---

## Key Features

- Bilingual throughout — Bangla + English side by side
- Automatic danger warning for interest rates above 25%
- WhatsApp and Facebook sharing of loan results
- Mobile responsive — works on low-end Android phones
- Language toggle — full Bangla mode for rural users
- Real statistics from MRA, Bangladesh Bank, World Bank
