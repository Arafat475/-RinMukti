# ঋণমুক্তি — RinMukti
### Financial Awareness Platform for Bangladesh

🌐 **Live Site:** https://arafat475.github.io/-RinMukti/index.html

---

## Why I Built This

Many families in Bangladesh go bankrupt because of loans they didn't fully understand. 
They don't know the real cost, the risks, or whether they even needed the loan in the 
first place. I built RinMukti to raise awareness and help people make informed financial 
decisions before it's too late.

---

## What It Does

**ঋণ ক্যালকুলেটর — Loan Calculator**
Calculate the real cost of any loan — monthly EMI, total interest, total repayment, 
and extra cost percentage. Includes an automatic danger warning for high interest rates 
common among NGOs and MFIs in Bangladesh.

**আমার কি ঋণ দরকার? — Do I Need This Loan?**
A 6-question quiz that helps people honestly assess whether they truly need a loan, 
with a scored verdict at the end.

People in Bangladesh — farmers, shopkeepers, families — who are considering taking
